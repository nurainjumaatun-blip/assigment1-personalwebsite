GET TO KNOW ME!

Hello! My name is Nur Batrisyia Iman Binti Azman, a 24-year-old student from Langkawi, Kedah.

I recently completed my Bachelor of Arts in Heritage Studies with Honours at Universiti Malaysia Kelantan (UMK). As someone who is passionate about history, culture, and heritage preservation, I have always been interested in learning more about museums and historical institutions.

At the moment, I am undergoing my practical training at Kota Kayang Museum, Perlis. This opportunity allows me to gain valuable hands-on experience in the museum field while learning from experienced professionals. Working in a museum environment has helped me deepen my understanding of heritage management and strengthened my passion for preserving history and culture for future generations.

I believe that every historical artifact and cultural heritage carries a meaningful story, and I am excited to continue exploring and contributing to the heritage industry in the future.
