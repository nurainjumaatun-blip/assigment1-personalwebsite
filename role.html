ABOUT ROLE CONTACT

Career Background

I began my journey in the museum field through an official application to the headquarters of the Malaysian Museum Department (JMM). As a Heritage Studies graduate from Universiti Malaysia Kelantan (UMK), I developed a strong interest in history, culture, and heritage preservation throughout my academic journey.

During my studies, I was exposed to the importance of protecting cultural heritage, historical artifacts, and traditional values. I realized that heritage is not only about understanding history, but also about preserving identity and ensuring that future generations continue to appreciate their cultural roots. This passion inspired me to explore the museum and heritage industry more deeply.

Experience

Currently, I am undergoing practical training at Kota Kayang Museum, Perlis, where I have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in the museum field. Throughout my training, I have been involved in various tasks such as understanding gallery layouts, recognizing and documenting artifacts, assisting with exhibitions, and observing how museum staff manage visitors and daily operations.

Working in a museum environment has been both exciting and challenging. Adapting to a professional workplace, learning museum procedures, and interacting with visitors have helped me improve my communication and practical skills. I have also learned the importance of artifact conservation and the proper handling techniques required to preserve historical collections carefully and responsibly.

This experience has strengthened my passion for the heritage and museum industry, and I look forward to continuing my journey in preserving history, culture, and heritage for future generations.
